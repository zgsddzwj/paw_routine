FurryNote Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 25, 2026

FurryNote ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. Please read this policy carefully before using the app.

1. Information We Collect

To provide pet daily logging and health management services, we may collect the following information:

  • Pet Profile: Name, breed, gender, birth date, neuter status, and profile photo
  • Daily Records: Feeding, walking, water changes, medication, defecation times, notes, and photos
  • Health Records: Vaccination history, deworming records, checkups, weight data, medical photos, and document photos
  • App Settings: Reminder times, theme preferences
  • Device Info: iOS version, device model (for app optimization, not personally identifiable)

2. Data Storage

Your data is stored primarily on your device. We do not upload your pet data to our servers.

Specific storage methods:

  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device using Apple SwiftData
  • iCloud Sync: If you are signed in to iCloud, data syncs via Apple CloudKit across your devices
  • Photos: Pet profile photos, medical photos, and document photos are stored locally or in your iCloud

3. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device; unauthorized third parties cannot access it
  • iCloud sync data is protected by Apple's encryption technology
  • We do not sell, share, or disclose your pet data to any third party
  • Local notifications run entirely offline without network transmission

4. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases (FurryNote Pro unlock). Transactions are handled directly by Apple; we do not collect your payment information
  • Apple CloudKit: For iCloud data sync. CloudKit is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Apple UserNotifications: For local reminders, completely offline with no server communication

5. Local Notifications

This app uses iOS local notifications for feeding, walking, and deworming reminders. These notifications:

  • Are generated and scheduled entirely on your device
  • Do not require an internet connection
  • Do not send any information to external servers
  • Can be disabled at any time in system settings

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • View & Edit: You can view, modify, or delete any pet record at any time
  • Export: You can export all records as a CSV file (Pro feature)
  • Delete: Deleting a pet profile removes all related records; uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • iCloud Management: You can manage iCloud data storage or disable iCloud sync in iOS Settings

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you within the app or in update notes. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
